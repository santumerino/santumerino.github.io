YARG is out!!!

YARG, a game I've been working on for 3 years (on and off), is finally, finally out.

I still can't believe that I managed to do it. Before this, I'd only made small games and started, but never finished, big projects.

But now I can say that I've made an entire game by myself (plus help with music) from start to finish.

Of course, it's not over yet. I'll be fixing bugs that will inevitably pop up, and I'm also working on a new major feature...

...but that's for later. For now, I can finally say that I did it.

 

I'll make a postmortem or something like that later. I have a lot of thoughts, and no idea how to express them. So be on the lookout for that.
