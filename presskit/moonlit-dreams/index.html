Description

Moonlit Dreams is a block pushing game with overlapping levels, inside a dreamscape you'll never want to leave. Push blocks, open doors, traverse mazes, and then do it again across and between three layers of psyche, while exploring an odd dreamscape that's strangely familiar yet mysterious.

Features

  • Push blocks, open doors, traverse mazes, and then do it again across and between three layers of psyche.
  • Explore an odd dreamscape that's strangely familiar yet mysterious.
  • Meet many similarly odd characters who are kind of you in a way, or I guess they're really different facets of yourself, or maybe it's just random neurons flashing inside your brain, who can say?
  • A good amount of levels, times three, with lovingly-crafted puzzles and narrative moments all throughout.
  • Several different border and palette options for you to mix and match as you please.
  • A fun, not terribly short, not terribly long narrative puzzle gaming experience, all in a little four-colour pixel art package.
  • Full controller support, in addition to regular-degular keyboard controls.
