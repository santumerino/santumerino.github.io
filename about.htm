About Me!

Who even is this "Santum" guy?

Home Page / Games / Translation Work / About Me / Blog

Hi! I'm Santiago Merino, I was born and raised in 2003 in Argentina (where I currently live), and I've been playing videogames for so long I can't remember a time when I didn't.

I've always wanted to make my own games. I had many false starts, but in 2017 I entered a game jam for the first time, and I've been making games ever since. I even managed to release my first commercial game in 2022!

In 2020 I also started helping people with translating their games, volunteering to help with games like Portal Reloaded, Will you Snail?, and another game for which I am uncredited. I'm also currently pursuing a proper career in translation.

 

If you'd like to contact me, your best bet is @-ing me on Twitter! And if you have any business inquiries (I think that's what those are called), my e-mail's at santumdev (at) gmail (dot) com!
