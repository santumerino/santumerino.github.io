YARG is a one-screen rage platformer that wants you to succeed!

It includes 30+ levels, a built-in level editor, Rage Mode (can you beat the entire game in one go with just 6 lives?), a story about AI and empathy, and so many secrets...

The game was released on September 16th 2022, and is available on Steam and Itch.io!
